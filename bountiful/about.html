Who we are

In the heart of Southern California, our fruit drink business thrives amidst the sun-soaked landscapes and coastal allure. Rooted in a passion for blending taste with the West Coast spirit, we meticulously craft each bottle to capture the essence of SoCal's golden vibes, sourcing the freshest blueberries, guavas, and peaches.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound — to offer a sip of Southern California in every bottle. We strive to create drinks that embody the region's diverse cultures, celebrating its rich agricultural abundance. From beachside gatherings to urban cafes, our fruit drinks have become synonymous with the SoCal lifestyle, spreading the taste of sun, surf, and laid-back living to all who seek a moment of bliss.

Carlsbad

Carlsbad, located in Southern California, is a coastal city renowned for its picturesque landscapes and laid-back atmosphere. Nestled along the Pacific Ocean, Carlsbad boasts stunning sandy beaches that draw residents and visitors alike. The city is known for its charming village atmosphere, offering a blend of boutique shops, delightful restaurants, and cultural attractions.

Visitors can explore the historic Carlsbad Village, where tree-lined streets and Spanish-style architecture create a quaint and inviting ambiance. In addition to its coastal charm, Carlsbad is celebrated for its family-friendly attractions. LEGOLAND California Resort, a popular theme park built entirely from LEGO bricks, adds a playful touch to the city's character.
