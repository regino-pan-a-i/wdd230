La Jolla Drink

Introducing La Jolla, a refreshing fruity concoction inspired by the sun-kissed shores of its namesake. Crafted to capture the essence of coastal charm and vibrant energy, this drink is the perfect companion for those seeking a taste of pure refreshment. Our carefully curated blend features the succulent flavors of blueberries, tropical guava, and sweet peaches. Each sip is a journey to summer, where the sun bathes the shores in golden warmth. The antioxidant-rich blueberries, tropical twist of guava, and juicy sweetness of peaches create a harmonious medley that embodies the spirit of La Jolla's coastal paradise. Whether you're by the pool, on the beach, or simply yearning for a sip of paradise, La Jolla is your go-to drink to savor the flavors of coastal bliss in every refreshing drop. Cheers to the vibrant energy and the taste of summer captured in a glass!.

Carlsbad

Carlsbad, located in Southern California, is a coastal city renowned for its picturesque landscapes and laid-back atmosphere. The city is known for its charming village atmosphere, offering a blend of boutique shops, delightful restaurants, and cultural attractions.
