City: Rexburg, Idaho

Overview

Purpose

This website will provide information about local businesses and events to help people get more familiar with what the city has to offer.

Audience

Anyone that is traveling to, lives in, or plans to move to the city.

Scenarios

  • Local residents want to know what christmas events there will be this season
  • A start up business is trying to reach more people and decide to join the chamber of commerce
  • A resident is looking for job opportunities in the city
